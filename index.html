Overwatch: The Beginner's Guide to Support Heroes

Welcome to the world of Overwatch, an online first-person shooter game built by Activision Blizzard. Whether you are an avid gamer and have several years of fps gaming under your belt, learning a new game with 30+ heroes can be tricky. This guide serves to provide a quick look into the Blizzard world of Overwatch and how to pick a support hero that fits your interests.
